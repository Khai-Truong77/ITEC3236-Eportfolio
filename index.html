Interactive Digital Media

As I have taken this course, I have learned many techniques and expanded my understanding of how people retain or learn information through different experiences. The process of consuming information has traditionally been a bleak experience filled with one off gimmicks in efforts to retain content. Through interactive media, users are able to interact, consume, and retain information at new levels thanks to new found ways to appeal to pathos. I hope you enjoy a peak into the methods and techniques I have learned to showcase my understanding of Interactive Digital Media.

Audio

Audio is a particular form on interactive digital media that I find special. While other nuances such as fonts and colors do well at visually evoking pathos, sounds can bring someone back to their roots and invoke feelings of comfort which make it easier to convey a message. In my particular podcast, I used sounds that an Asian American might here while growing up. The sounds of Gongs, Chimes, and background music that one might hear in a travel vlog that covers asian countries. It was fun choosing amongst thousands of sound bytes to determine which sounds to layer over my podcast to give it a cultural feel to better showcase my topic. Many modern podcasts will add sound effects and background music to fill the void of silence and create emphasis on major talking points.
